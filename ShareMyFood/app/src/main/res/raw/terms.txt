How to share my food ?
1) Just click on the + sign at the bottom of the screen.
2) Snap a yummy photo and upload it.
3) Enter basic information about the food to help others mouth water.
4) Set up your pick up location for delivery and maintain your availability.
5) ...And Share.
6) Once the food is delivered, just click on "Food Delivered" so that the listing is invisible to others.

How to get the shared food ?
1) On the home screen, click on the Filter icon to the top right and set the distance you are comfortable travelling to get the shared food.
2) All the foods shared will be visible by clicking on the Apple icon at the bottom left.
3) Just click on the food you like to consume and click on Message to co-ordinate with the provider.
